The Legacy

Pokémon was created by Satoshi Tajiri in 1995, (21 years ago) and and is centered on fictional creatures called "Pokémon", which humans, known as Pokémon Trainers, catch and train to battle each other for sport. Originally there were 151 Pokémon. Since then five more "generations" of Pokémon have been released.
